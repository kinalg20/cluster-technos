ITeS Services

(INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY enabled SERVICES)

(ITES) information technology-enabled services also termed as a web enabled services or remote services. In this era of digitalization information technology-enabled services house the complete range of operations that utilize information technology for redefining the efficacy and accuracy of the operations by simplifying the working and improving the functioning of the business by the outsourcing of numerous processes through external agencies mainly by companies in non-information technology fields./p>

MEDICAL TRANSCRIPTION : Having a stressful job and taking out time for transcribing gets almost impossible for any medical practitioner because it is important but not the core operation. That’s why outsourcing transcription services is the best solution here through any external agency where a transcriptionist is trained in transcribing different kinds of medical documents, reports, and audio files and then converted into accurate transcripts, formatted exactly how it needs to be.

DOCUMENT PROCESSING : For any business or organization handling the manual form of files is a headache but with advancements in technology transforming the manual forms and analogue data into a digital format is getting towards automation as robotic and artificial intelligence document processing this way the process of document processing becomes easy through outsourcing.

DATA ENTRY AND PROCESSING : Data entry means continuous warehousing and maintaining the database records with the latest and accurate information of an organization. And when a business has warehoused enough data for the processing it means alteration and manipulation of the collected and maintained data to obtain better quality results to attract the desired clients.

DATA WAREHOUSING : In the present scenario, if you want to grow your business and attract the right type of customers, who are actually in need of your product then data is the key. As your business grows you have your hands on the data of your previous clients and when stored it can be used to build the future course of action as data warehouses are simply intended to perform analysis and scrutiny for planning and taking action.

IT HELP DESK SERVICES : Help desk services are important to business but are not the core function, it requires the engagement of business resources that can be used somewhere else and also hampers the customer reach, so nowadays IT helpdesk services are in a market you can outsource easily and don’t need any engagement. It is easy to approach a communication centre that provides a single point of contact between a company, its customers, employees and business partners. It promotes flawless functioning by solving real-time issues. The IT service desk assists throughout the entire product lifecycle and ensures that all users receive help readily without any delay.

APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT : Because it is the digital era every business needs an online presence to reach out to the maximum audience, build a chain of potential clients and get maximum conversions for having a robust and attractive application that must perform various required tasks of an organization. And every app-building process involves gathering requirements, designing prototypes, testing, implementing, and integrating which is better outsourced by third-party experts in the field.

ENTERPRISE RESOURCE PLANNING : It was only yesterday when resource planning was a time consuming and heavy task for the workforce of the business, it used to need hard and conscious work for a very long period but as time has passed, today things are different, certain third-party software is there in the market which a business can take advantage of and create a simple and easy resource planning at the comfort of working remotely which includes accounting, planning, sales, marketing, finance, human resources, project management, risk management and compliance, supply chain operations, the free flow of communication between business areas, accurate and real-time data reporting.

TECHNICAL OPERATION SUPPORT :There are various technical operations happening in a business at a moment from building the product to successful delivery to the client and those operations should always run smoothly for which a team of experts must always stay on their feet to provide support in managing, maintaining, repairing IT systems, diagnosing and repairing faults, resolving network issues, and installing and configuring hardware and software without any delay. And it is always better to outsource that team of experts to have better results and cost-effectiveness.

PRODUCT TECHNICAL MAINTAINANCE :When any company or business is focusing on the core operations of that business and doesn’t have experts in the organization to look into certain issues of maintenance or repair of the technical system of the product, there outsourcing is the right way to take. Product technical maintenance is the operation of the business which involves functional checks, servicing, repairing or replacing of necessary devices or parts, equipment and machinery, building infrastructure, and supporting utilities in industrial, business, and residential installations etc done by a third party.
